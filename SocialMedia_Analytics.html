#Total Likes

def human_format(num):
    for unit in ['', 'K', 'M', 'B']:
        if abs(num) < 1000:
            return f"{num:.0f}{unit}"
        num /= 1000
    return f"{num:.1f}B"

fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(8,5))
platform_stats.plot(
    x='Platform',
    y=['Likes'],
    kind='bar',
    color='black',
    edgecolor='black',
    ax=ax
)

# Add human-readable labels
for container in ax.containers:
    labels = [human_format(v) for v in container.datavalues]
    ax.bar_label(container, labels=labels, fontsize=9, fontweight='bold')

plt.title('Total Likes & Comments by Platform', fontsize=14)
plt.xlabel('Platform')
plt.ylabel('Likes')
plt.xticks(rotation=45)
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()

📲 Social Media Engagement Project – Insight Summary¶

🧹 Data Overview:¶

  • The dataset contains 8,000+ social media posts across platforms like Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, and TikTok.
  • Each post has attributes such as Post Date, Platform, Hashtag, Content Type, Region, Views, Likes, Shares, and Comments.
  • Engagement levels were standardized for meaningful comparison.

🔍 EDA Insights:¶

1. 📊 Engagement by Platform¶

  • Instagram has the highest overall engagement rate (~13.82%).
  • YouTube follows closely with ~13.10%.
  • Twitter and TikTok are slightly lower (~12.7–12.8%).

2. 👍 Total Likes & Comments¶

  • Instagram dominates with the most likes and comments across all platforms.
  • YouTube also performs strongly in both metrics.
  • Twitter maintains a balanced like-to-comment ratio.

3. 🎥 Content Type Trends¶

  • Posts form the majority of the dataset and drive the highest engagement.
  • Live Streams on YouTube and Instagram receive strong but niche engagement.
  • Stories/Reels (where available) contribute meaningfully to user interaction.

4. #️⃣ Hashtag Usage¶

  • Hashtags are sometimes duplicated in lowercase/uppercase (e.g., #Fitness vs #fitness).
  • Normalizing hashtags reduces duplication and shows that #fitness, #music, and #comedy are among the most engaging tags.

5. 🌍 Regional Performance¶

  • The USA and UK lead in engagement volume.
  • Brazil and Canada show strong interaction rates relative to their post counts.
  • Regional differences indicate localized content strategies are effective.

🧠 Key Takeaways:¶

  • Instagram is the best platform for driving user engagement.
  • YouTube continues to be a reliable platform for both views and interactions.
  • Standardizing hashtags can improve campaign measurement.
  • Focus marketing strategies on high-performing regions (USA, UK, Brazil).
  • Experiment with content diversity (Posts, Live Streams, Stories) for maximizing reach.
